THE STORY OF CRICKET🏏🏏🏏


Cricket grew out of many stick - and - ball games played in England 500 years ago ,under
a variety of different roles.The word "bat" is an old English word that simply means
stick or club . By the seventeenth Century Cricket had evolved enough to be recognisable
as a distinct game and it was popular enough for its fans to be fined for playing it on
Sundays instead of going to Church . Till the middle of the eighteen century, bats were
roughly the same shape as hockey sticks , curving towards at the bottom .There was a
simple reason for this : the ball was bowled underarm , along the ground and the curve at

the end bat gave the Batsman the best chance of making contact . google.
